How the game works.

  • It is a simple game for 2 players: user vs computer.
  • User and Computer picks rock, paper, or scissors together at the same time.
  • Scissors will beat paper, but loses to rock
  • Paper will beat rock, but loses to scissors.
  • Rock will beat scissors, but loses to paper.
If you are interested in the serious histroy of this game - Check out Rock-Paper-Scissors on Wikipedia.
